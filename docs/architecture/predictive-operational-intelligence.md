# Predictive Operational Intelligence

## Operational foresight philosophy
This layer performs deterministic operational trajectory inference (not statistical prophecy), grounding every forecast in continuity retrieval and cross-domain correlation evidence.

## Core capabilities
- Trajectory forecasting across delivery, governance, stakeholder, procurement, financial, technical, and overall domains.
- Scenario projections for no intervention and intervention-aware paths.
- Intervention impact estimates with pressure and recovery deltas.
- Confidence scoring with dampers and explicit uncertainty reasons.
- Anti-noise controls (deduplication + minimum confidence threshold) to reduce unsupported claims.
- Predictive risk clusters and deterministic narratives for executive and copilot consumption.

## Integration points
- PM copilot and context APIs can call `retrievePredictiveOperationalIntelligence` for foresight payloads.
- Executive digest and recovery summaries can consume outcome + scenario + narrative subsets.
- Governance boundaries are preserved via operational scope validation before context assembly.
