# Operational Continuity Retrieval

This layer implements deterministic operational continuity cognition: it prioritizes unresolved pressure, reconstructs causality lineage, computes operational atmosphere, and emits explainable diagnostics while preserving strict scope isolation.

## Core features
- Prioritization of unresolved blockers, escalation recurrence, governance gaps, and delivery dependencies.
- Weighted continuity scoring with unresolved age amplification.
- Lineage graph reconstruction (ancestry edges via `parentRecordId` + `lineageType`).
- Timeline narrative reconstruction for continuity-critical events.
- Stakeholder continuity indicators (silence, escalation participation, unresolved ownership).
- Intervention continuity indicators (failed attempt recurrence and exhaustion).
- Atmosphere model with trajectory + degradation indicators.
- Anti-noise controls for duplicate suppression, stale damping, and low-confidence filtering.
- Deterministic diagnostics describing why items and atmosphere were prioritized.
