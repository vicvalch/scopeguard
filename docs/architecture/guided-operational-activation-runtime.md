# Guided Operational Activation Runtime

## Philosophy
PMFreak onboarding is implemented as operational awakening. The runtime advances as operational evidence accumulates: context ingestion, continuity retrieval, stakeholder dependency interpretation, risk cognition, and executive synthesis.

## Aha-Moment Semantics
Aha moments are inferred from behavior: repeated continuity retrieval, memory reuse, multi-session continuity, executive synthesis engagement, and ingestion recurrence. They are deterministic runtime detections, not UI toggles.

## Activation Runtime Model
The `OperationalActivationRuntimeState` holds progression stage, activation/readiness/confidence scores, continuity confidence, operational maturity, contextual actions, progression blockers/dependencies, behavioral/complexity signals, and runtime cursor metadata for replay safety.

## Milestones
Milestones are operational cognition transitions (for example first continuity retrieval, executive awareness activation, operational intelligence activation), each carrying replay-safe identity and progression impact semantics.

## Scoring Rationale
Scores are explainable composites designed for deterministic evolution across sessions: activation, readiness, continuity depth, engagement intensity, operational maturity, enterprise expansion, executive awareness, and aha confidence.

## Guidance and Nudging
Guidance is generated from runtime state and milestone/score posture, producing contextual operational recommendations. Nudges prioritize continuity activation, unresolved risk review, stakeholder dependency review, and executive synthesis generation.

## Metering Relationship
Activation integrates metering posture through quota evaluation and activation-aware consumption interpretation. Runtime decisions account for soft/hard exhaustion, burst allowance, and temporary expansion while preserving provider-independent semantics.

## Enterprise Evolution
The progression engine supports individual, enterprise, reactivation, and recovery paths. This prepares PMO onboarding, delegated activation, collaborative continuity, and organization-wide operational maturity assessment.

## Replay and Integrity
Transition chain preparation includes `previousTransitionHash` and `transitionChainHash`, deterministic capability snapshot hashing, immutable transition construction, and cursored runtime progression.
