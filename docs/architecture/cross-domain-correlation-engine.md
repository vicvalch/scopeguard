# Cross-Domain Correlation Engine

The cross-domain correlation engine introduces deterministic operational convergence cognition across financial, technical, stakeholder, delivery, governance, and procurement domains.

## Core capabilities
- Normalizes operational memory records into `CrossDomainSignal` records with domain, severity, confidence, unresolved state, and pressure contribution.
- Detects convergence patterns where multi-domain degradation reinforces instability.
- Models pressure propagation across domains to preserve causality and escalation lineage.
- Reconstructs correlated clusters and a lineage graph for explainable diagnostics.
- Computes systemic operational atmosphere with fragility, collapse probability, and recovery probability.
- Applies anti-noise controls with confidence thresholding, duplicate suppression, and stale damping.

## Operational cognition outcomes
- Convergence detection for systemic risk amplification.
- Systemic instability indicators when weak signals converge.
- Recovery probability and delivery survivability indicators for executive prioritization.
- Correlated narratives and diagnostics suitable for copilot and executive digests.
