# Executive Operational Command Runtime

## Philosophy
Evidence-backed deterministic orchestration layer for executive stabilization decisions; avoids KPI theater, fake certainty, and black-box scoring.

## Stabilization orchestration
Synthesizes continuity, correlation, predictive, critical path, intervention, and learning signals into executive priorities.

## Portfolio synthesis
Computes portfolio instability, shared bottlenecks, governance recurrence, procurement pressure, PM saturation, and drift.

## Instability zones
Detects localized/systemic/spreading concentration zones and bottlenecks.

## Survivability orchestration
Computes delivery, milestone, operational, recovery, and portfolio survivability with explicit can-stabilize signal.

## Escalation saturation
Surfaces overload, fatigue, ineffective loops, executive saturation, and governance congestion.

## PM overload
Models PM overload, intervention overload, coordination saturation, fragmentation, recovery fatigue, and escalation burden.

## Anti-theater safeguards
Requires evidence arrays, bounded confidence values, uncertainty notes, deterministic weighting, causality explanations.

## Governance safety
Enforces tenant/workspace scope presence and includes governance boundaries in every executive artifact.
