# Purpose

Risk Center is PMFreak's proactive intelligence hub for identifying, scoring, and managing threats to delivery confidence and strategic outcomes.

# Core Entities

- Risk Item
- Risk Score
- Mitigation Plan
- Trigger Condition
- Residual Exposure

# Primary Workflows

- Capture and categorize new risks with impact and probability context.
- Prioritize mitigation actions and assign accountable owners.
- Monitor trigger conditions and revise exposure as projects evolve.

# AI Behaviors

- Infer emerging risks from cross-module telemetry.
- Re-score risk exposure based on live project movement.
- Recommend mitigation actions with expected risk reduction.

# Operational Signals

- Risk accumulation in specific portfolios or teams.
- Mitigation plans past due or ineffective over time.
- Trigger events indicating imminent delivery degradation.

# UX Notes

Risk interaction should feel analytical but actionable. Users should move from detection to mitigation in minimal steps, with clear confidence indicators and auditability.

# Visual Direction

Matrix-style scoring views, trend lines, and severity-first visual encoding. Detail panels should surface history, rationale, and mitigation ownership clearly.

# Future Runtime Behaviors

The runtime should support adaptive risk models, automatic trigger surveillance, and workflow escalation to Command Center when exposure crosses governance thresholds.
